What is Bazi?

The Four Pillars of Destiny

Bazi (八字), or Four Pillars of Destiny, is a Chinese metaphysical system based on your birth date and time. It maps the balance of the five elements in your life — Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water — and how they interact with your luck cycles.

🌱

Year Pillar

🌙

Parents, career path, and the core of your adult life.

Day Pillar

Your self — personality, relationships, and marriage.

⏰

Hour Pillar

Children, later life, and your hidden talents.
